Zaire's Kitchadi Soup Recipe

This is ayuveda recipe that I learned about through a ayuveda recipe book. I think of it like an ayuveda version of chicken noodle soup, since it's great to eat when you're feeling sick.

Mixing spices for the curry

Gather and mix these spices into a bowl before you add them to your soup. Make sure to set the ghee aside until you're ready to heat your pot and add these spices.

  • 2 Tbs. ghee
  • 1 Tbs. cumin seeds
  • 1 Tbs. tumeric
  • 1 Tbs. ground corriander
  • 1 Tbs. freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 Tbs. ground cinnamon
  • 1 Tbs. ground cumin
  • 1/2 Tbs. sea salt
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 2 Tbs. minced fresh ginger
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced

Ingredients for the soup

Once you've added your spices you'll add these Ingredients. The chicken can be prepared and added to the soup after it's cooked

  • 1 onion diced
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/2 cup of dried split mung beans
  • 1/2 cup of shelled peas
  • 1 cup chopped asparagus
  • 1 cup chopped butternut squash
  • 1 cup kale
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 sliced chicken breasts
